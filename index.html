About

This tool helps you create memorable but strong passwords, checks password strength, and demonstrates (safely) how an attacker might attempt to break weak passwords. (This is a demo — do not paste real high-value passwords here.)

Why strong passwords matter

Passwords remain a primary control for protecting accounts. Weak or reused passwords are a top cause of breaches — a strong unique password per account is essential.

Cybercrime snapshot

  • Weak/reused passwords are commonly exploited in account takeover attacks.
  • Use a unique, long password or a passphrase for important accounts.
